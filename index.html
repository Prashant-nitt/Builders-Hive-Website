About us

Builders'​ Hive is the Social Innovation and Engineering club of NIT-Trichy (NIT-T). We, the Builders'​ Hive address the problems surrounding the society and the environment through the eyes of science, technology, rationality and infuse that extra spark of creativity in coming up with sustainable solutions for the benefit of all.

Our Articles & Posters

Every topic is like a coin. It has two sides addressing it with varying perspectives that meet at a point. The idea of human evolution also has two interesting and varying ideas, where one faction tends to find newer boundaries to scale, while the other wants to make things better in the comfort zone it already is in. The Colonization of Mars is a thought held dear by a lot of people belonging to the former and we the Builders' Hive present a miniscule poster report on the first steps taken in order to start and sustain life in the Red Planet.

But, should Colonization of Mars be the next goal that we humans should invest our technology and thoughts into? Or should we spend more of our resources into making this one home that we have always had, the Earth into a better place? Whose side are you in?

by Builders' Hive , 26/03/2018
